The idea

We took the volume
apart.

Every keyboard hides its construction inside one opaque shell. We cut that volume into horizontal layers and made each layer a colour you can see through. Scroll, and the block comes apart.

PLATE 03 / MAGENTA / structure

STACKED

The pattern on top isn't printed on. It's the sum of what's underneath.

Screens add light to make colour. Paper takes it away. FOURPLATE is built on the second rule: each plate filters the light passing through the ones above it.

  • PlatesFour, translucent cast, one per colour
  • MountFull-stack gasket
  • RegistrationAligned to a single optical axis

Construction

Four layers.
One of them you touch.

Three plates carry the structure, the cavity and the electronics. The top is drilled for your switches and keycaps. The whole stack is the gasket, so the board gives as you type and sounds like the layers it's built from.
