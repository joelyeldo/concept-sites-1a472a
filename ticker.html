The premise

Nobody knows what they're
actually spending on AI.

It leaves your account in tiny invisible sips. A subscription here, an API key there, three agents running while you sleep. The number only shows up once it has already hurt.

TICKER makes it loud. It takes the spend you cannot see and prints it into something you can hold, pin to a wall, and post.

How it works

Four moves. One ritual.

  1. 01

    Load the roll

    Drop in a roll of paper like it's 1998. The satisfying part is the point.

  2. 02

    Link your accounts

    Connect every model, agent and API key you pretend you're not using.

  3. 03

    Pick a mode

    By model, by project, daily, weekly. Or whenever you need a reality check.

  4. 04

    Hit print

    Out comes the damage, itemized, in colour, with commentary you didn't ask for.

Every model gets its own receipt.

Claude prints orange. The rest get their own colours, logos and quietly judgmental tone. Pick a model, pick a confession, hit print.
